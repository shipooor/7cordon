Record 7cordon Demo Video

1. Open video.html in another tab first
2. Click on that tab once to activate audio
3. Come back here and click "Start Recording"
4. In the Chrome dialog, switch to "Chrome Tab" at the top
5. Select the "7cordon" tab
6. Make sure "Also share tab audio" is checked
7. Click "Share"
8. Press R to restart the presentation
9. Wait ~2 min, then click "Stop sharing" or wait for auto-stop
10. Video downloads automatically as .webm
